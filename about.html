About Me

Digital sorcerer weaving together experiences for humans, animals, plants, and the mystical realm.

I have recently graduated from the University of Texas at Austin 🤘Hook'em!🤠 with a B.F.A in Design 🎨. I have a minor in Informatics ℹ️ with a concentration in human-computer interaction and user-experience research. I also love to code and have studied Computer Science under the Elements of Computing Certification 🧩 track at UT.

At LIMITS 2024 (linked below) I presented my project on Digital Degrowth 🍀, in which I explored designing a personalized rehabilitation ritual targeting smart phone addiction. Aside from my scholarly ventures my hobbies include illustrating, video gaming, reading, watching anime, eating nigiri's, and drinking boba!
